Use This Key Framework for Structuring Real Estate Investments with Business Partners

By Symon He | in Online Courses

  • Description
  • Instructor
  • Specs

Splitting profits with partners isn't so cut-and-dry when it comes to investing in real estate. What if each person is contributing different amounts of capital or labor? What if one partner is contributing more money and zero labor? In situations like these and more, real estate professionals rely on the Waterfall Distribution Framework. In this course, you'll discover what the framework is and how it's used for investments; and you'll learn how it's applied with two example case studies to learn from.
